Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of courses does 14 Knowledge Lab offer?

14 Knowledge Lab offers a variety of courses focused on sports law, business, and technology. Our flagship course is the FIFA Agent Exam preparation, designed to help aspiring sports agents pass the official FIFA examination. We also offer specialized webinars, workshops, and continuous education programs for sports industry professionals.

What kind of devices do I need to access the courses and other content?

Our platform is accessible on any device with an internet connection, including desktop computers, laptops, tablets, and smartphones. For the best experience, we recommend using our mobile app, available for both iOS and Android devices.

How are the courses delivered?

Our courses are delivered through a combination of pre-recorded video lectures, live webinars, interactive quizzes, and downloadable study materials. You'll have access to our online learning platform where all content is organized in a structured, easy-to-follow format. For certain premium courses, we also offer one-on-one mentoring sessions with industry experts.

How long do I have access to course materials?

Once you purchase a course, you'll have lifetime access to all the course materials. This allows you to learn at your own pace and revisit the content whenever you need a refresher. For subscription-based services, access is maintained as long as your subscription is active.

What is the 14th Update?

The 14th Update is our regular newsletter that keeps you informed about the latest developments in sports law, business trends, and technological innovations in the sports industry. Subscribers receive exclusive content, early access to new courses, and special discounts on our premium offerings.

What kind of certification can I earn by completing a 14 Knowledge Lab course?

Upon successful completion of our courses, you'll receive a digital certificate from 14 Knowledge Lab. While our courses are designed to prepare you for official examinations like the FIFA Agent Exam, the certificates themselves are for professional development and do not replace official licensing requirements.

How can I get in touch with 14 Knowledge Lab customer support?

You can reach our customer support team via email at info@14sportslaw.com. We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours. For urgent matters, you can request a callback through our website or mobile app.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. For certain courses, we also offer installment payment plans to make education more accessible.

Do I need prior experience in sports to join?

While some background knowledge in sports is beneficial, many of our courses are designed for beginners and provide all the necessary foundational knowledge. We welcome enthusiasts, students, and professionals at all stages of their careers.

What should I do if my query is not listed here?

If your question isn't addressed in our FAQ section, please contact us directly at info@14sportslaw.com or request a callback. Our team is always happy to assist with any specific inquiries you might have.
